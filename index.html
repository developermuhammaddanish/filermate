Tax Year 2024-2025

Income Range (PKR) Tax Rate
Up to 600,000 0%
600,001 - 1,200,000 5% on excess over 600,000

2,200,001 - 3,200,000 180,000 + 25% on excess over 2,200,000
Above 3,200,000 35% on excess over 3,200,000

Tax Year 2025-2026

Income Range (PKR) Tax Rate
Up to 600,000 0%
600,001 - 1,200,000 5% on excess over 600,000
1,200,001 - 2,400,000 30,000 + 10% on excess over 1,200,000
2,400,001 - 3,200,000 150,000 + 15% on excess over 2,400,000
3,200,001 - 4,100,000 345,000 + 30% on excess over 3,200,000
Above 4,100,000 615,000 + 35% on excess over 4,100,000
